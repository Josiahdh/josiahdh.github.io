### Built as a part of my Design for Interactive Media course at USC, Robodog is an isometric puzzle game about wordplay!

---

Created in five weeks with Yanzhi Yang, Sherry Xie, and Peilin He, my role focused on ensuring our puzzles were mechanically and conceptually consistent. I also lead our team’s documentation and organization.

![Image](/assets/images/robodog/expo-picture.jpg)

Robodog was featured in the 2025 USC Games Expo! Thanks to all our players for keeping our booth busy the entire event!
